About Us

We are proud to introduce ourselves as the leading trade association of IT and Telecom Suppliers of Central India under the banner MPCTA which stands for Malwa Personal  Computer Telecom Association.

Our current membership strength is 150+ and 10+ corporate members which is growing on year to year basis

MPCTA has over years addressed many problems and threats related to Info-tech Industry in Central India. It has provided an active platform to its members, IT Companies, IT Vendors, and Service Providers for interaction to the benefit of Industry. It has also played an important role in improving honest trade practices, removing piracy, training of its members on new technologies etc.
